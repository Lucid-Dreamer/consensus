\renewcommand{\Moves}{
  \DEFAULTMOVE{Pupils}{You have a number of students who follow your
    mystical and magical teachings. At the \TRIGGER{beginning of the
      session}, roll +learning to determine how their studies are
      progressing. On a 10+ they have a surplus. On a 7-9, both a
      surplus and a want. On a 6-, they will do everything in their
      power to satisfy all their wants.}
  \MOVE{Meditation}{When you \TRIGGER{meditate on the best course of
      action} for at least an hour, roll +Wits. On a 10+ hold 3, on a
    7-9 hold 1. You may spend this hold 1-for-1 to ask the GM a
    question from this list, the GM will answer truthfully. You take
    +1 forward when acting on this information.
    \begin{itemize}
    \item What's my escape route? 
    \item How dangerous is this plan? 
    \item What here is most useful to me? 
    \item What here is the most dangerous?
    \end{itemize}
  }

\renewcommand{\PaneFive}{\SECTION{Your Students:}
  By default you have about 10 students, they are moderately studious
  sleepers and they respect you, though they're not fully loyal. They
  have their own lives apart from you, family and friends in the
  area. (learning +1. surplus: time. want: departure.)

  Pick up to two of these:
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Your students are competitive (replace want: departure with
    want: competition)
  \item Your students are dedicated (replace surplus: time with
    surplus: advanced students)
  \item Your students are driven (replace surplus: time with surplus:
    dedication)
  \item Your students are lazy (replace want: departure with want:
  sloth)
  \end{itemize}

  And one of these:
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Your school is famous (add surplus: growth and want: infamy)
  \item Your school is large (default students 20)
  \item Your students are quick learners (+1 learning)
  \item You have a protage.
  \item Your most advanced student is Awakened 
  \item Your students are rowdy (add want: violence and surplus:
    intimidation)
  \end{itemize}
}
