\renewcommand{\MoveSpacing}{-.25em}
\renewcommand{\Moves}{
  \DEFAULTMOVE{Exactly When I Mean To}{At the \textbf{beginning of the
      session}, roll+Grace. On a 10+, hold 2. On a 7–9, hold 1. At any
    time, either you or the GM can spend your hold to have you arrive
    in a scene, with the proper tools and knowledge, with or without
    any clear explanation why. Take +1 forward when you do so. On a
    miss, the GM holds 1, and can spend it to have you arrive, but
    somehow pinned, caught or trapped}
  \MOVE{Call Through Shadow}{When you call a mundane or common object
    to yourself, roll +Will. On a hit, you have successfully summoned
    it to you. On a 10+ you're in the clear. On a 7-9 choose 1:
    \vspace{-.5em}
    \begin{itemize}
      \setlength\itemsep{-.5em}
    \item It comes out of a heated nearby situation
    \item It comes with strings attached
    \item It can be traced back to you
    \end{itemize}
  }
  \MOVE{Scrying Bowl}{When you have an object related to a person and
    use it to gaze upon their current situation, roll +Wits. On a 10+
    all 3, on a 7-9 choose 1:
    \vspace{-.5em}
    \begin{itemize}
      \setlength\itemsep{-.5em}
    \item They don't know they're being watched
    \item You can identify where they are
    \item You can discern what they are doing
    \end{itemize}
  }
  \MOVE{If You Ask Me}{When another character comes to you for advice
    and you \textbf{tell them honestly what you think the best course of
    action is}, they take +1 ongoing while following your advice, and
    you mark experience.}
  \MOVE{Magical Experimentation}{ When you attempt to push your magic
    past its current boundaries in a controlled setting, roll
    +Body. On a 10+ all 3, on a 7-9 choose 1:
    \begin{itemize}
      \setlength\itemsep{-.5em}
    \item It works pretty much as intended
    \item It doesn't cause a scene/draw extra attention
    \item It was controlled enough that you can repeat it
    \end{itemize}
  }
  \MOVE{This One Is Mine}{When you \textbf{engage another magical
      being in a duel}, so long as your companions don’t interfere,
    others will give you enough space to finish it, come what may.}
}
